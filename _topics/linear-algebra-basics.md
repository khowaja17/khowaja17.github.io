Here's the column-space / row-space intuition.

**Projection formula:** For unit vector $\hat u$,
$$
\mathrm{proj}_{\hat u}(x) = (\hat u^\top x)\, \hat u.
$$

**Orthogonal projector:** $P = A(A^\top A)^{-1}A^{-1}A^\top$ when $A$ has full column rank.
